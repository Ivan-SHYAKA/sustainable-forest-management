How can technology aid in forest management 

Forest management is a crucial aspect that needs management and control because it is an essential part in ensuring the air is clean and the weather as well as other environmental factors are well regulated and behaving at normal rate. unfortunately our forests face several dangers, either human endangerment or other natural catastrophes/disasters. Technology can help in the management of forests in several ways:

1. Through technology, we can predict extreme weather changes that can contribute to the endangerment of the forest. if accurate and precise predictions are performed; appropriate measures can be taken accordingly which can reduce these unusual  circumstances affecting forests.

2. a method of electric fencing and registration of every forest entry can help ensure that forests are accessed by only authorized personnel and also keep track of other individuals entering forests, this can allow forest managers to keep track of individuals that can access forests and can reduce a number of individuals doing afforestation.

3. sensor devices can be put into every tree in the forest, these sensors can allow tracking the life/wellbeing of each tree in the forest. and can provide live alerts in case of deforestation, and daily reporting of each tree's status present in the forest.
